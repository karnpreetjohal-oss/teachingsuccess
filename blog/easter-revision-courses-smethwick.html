What Good Tuition Should Include

  • Initial baseline assessment and realistic next-step targets
  • Weekly lesson structure with independent follow-up tasks
  • Clear marking and progress visibility for parents
  • Exam-board alignment where relevant (AQA, Edexcel, OCR)

Common Mistakes To Avoid

Parents often choose purely on price or convenience. A better approach is to check teaching quality, consistency of feedback, and how quickly weak topics are identified and fixed.

Simple Weekly Plan

Use one focused tutoring session, one targeted homework set, and one short review/check-in each week. This keeps momentum high without overwhelming the student.
