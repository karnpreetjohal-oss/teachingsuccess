How to Choose the Right Tutor

Prioritise lesson quality, consistency, and accountability. The best tutoring setup includes a clear baseline, weekly targets, and straightforward reporting so parents can see exactly what is improving.

Core Elements of High-Quality Tuition

  • Initial diagnostic check and realistic targets
  • Structured one-to-one sessions matched to student level
  • Independent follow-up tasks between lessons
  • Progress review every 4 weeks with next actions
